Hafiz is a Postdoctoral Research Associate (PDRA) in the Department of Mechanical Engineering, Thermofluids Division at Imperial College London.

He is working in Hazelab in the area of smouldering Combustion, in the ERC funded project HAZE. He is involved in lab scale and field scale experiments and his work focuses on understanding the smouldering combustion dynamics using optical diagnostics.
